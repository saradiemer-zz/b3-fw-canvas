If you plan to finance remaining costs with a loan, options are available:

    Parent Loan for Undergraduate Students
  • Borrowed from the federal government by a parent(s)/guardian(s)
  • Repayable 10 years after the loan is applied to the student account.

    Alternate student loan
  • Financed through private lenders
  • Repayable over 10 years, beginning six months after you are no longer enrolled at least part-time
  • Most undergraduate students need a co-signer.

    iPlan
  • (Tip) Keep us informed: We may be able to re-evaluate your financial aid award if your family experiences a substantial change in circumstances that affects your financial situation for the academic year.
