Visiting Both the Lake Shore and Water Tower Campuses

We encourage you to visit both campuses, especially if your areas of interest include these majors located downtown: Schools of Education, Social Work, Business Administration, and Communication, as well as majors in computer science and criminal justice. Ideal itineraries for visitng both campuses are:

  • 10am Lake Shore visit (10am-12pm) followed by 2:15pm Water Tower Tour (2:15pm-3:00pm)
    OR
  • 12pm Water Tower tour (12pm-12:45pm) followed by Lake Shore visit (2pm-4pm)

During the fall and spring semester, when classes are in session we have Loyola shuttle passes available in the Undergraduate Admission Office. If the shuttle is not running, we are happy to provide driving or public transportation directions.

If you need disability-related accommodations, please contact us at visitloyola@luc.edu.
