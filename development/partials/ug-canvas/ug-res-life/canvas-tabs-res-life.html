First-year housing

Residence halls are where you'll meet your new friends and create some of your fondest memories. And, first-year students, we have some nice amenities for you. All first-year halls have air conditioning, internet access, laundry rooms, study and social lounges, a 24-hour reception desk.

International house

The mission of the International House is to promote cross-cultural learning by creating a community in which students live and interact with other students from different countries. We have had students from over 26 different countries, including Brazil, China, Japan, Spain, Ireland, and Angola.
