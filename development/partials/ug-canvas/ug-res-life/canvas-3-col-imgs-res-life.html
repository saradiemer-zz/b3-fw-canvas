What are the dorms like?
Traditional

This is your typical dorm with community bathrooms. We offer triple-, double-, and limited single-occupancy rooms.

Suite-style

You'll have a roomate(s) and suitemates. And you won't have to share a bathroom with the whole floor, so that's pretty cool.

Private

There's a private bathroom in your dorm room. Sounds pretty unheard of, but we like that amentity and think you will too.
