Why Loyola?

A Loyola education means you’ll be transformed. Where does it begin? Is it on the quad? Or in the halls? Or by the lake? Is it on a trip to Rome? Or the streets of Chicago? Truth be told, it’s everywhere. And it all starts with you. Learn more

College with a conscience

Social justice isn’t just an ideal. It’s a something our faculty and students work toward every day at Loyola. It’s a something you can be a part of too, before and after graduation. Learn more
