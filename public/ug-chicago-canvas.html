Our home is as big as your dreams

At Loyola, a world-class city serves as your extended campus. Chicago is second to none in offering the job opportunities, internships and cultural experiences that bring the classroom into the real world. And did we mention how much fun you'll have in the process?

Located in Rogers Park, Loyola’s Lake Shore Campus is just a short train-ride from downtown Chicago and countless opportunities.

Our Water Tower Campus is nestled right in the heart of Chicago’s busy downtown—and steps from the pretty magnificent “Magnificent Mile.”

Sometimes, the Lake Shore Campus seems a part of a completely different city. Students have the chance to truly feel like members of the community.

Getting back and forth between campuses is easy: Besides the ‘L’ trains, students can take Loyola’s intercampus shuttle or one of many city buses.

Navy Pier might be one of Chicago’s most iconic landmarks, but that doesn’t mean it’s the only one. Explore, and you might just discover a new one yourself.

Loyola has two lakeside campuses, and we like to make the most of them. Start with a bike ride on the Lakefront Trail and end with a day at the beach.
